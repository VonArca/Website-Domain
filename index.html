I am Tokwa

I specialize in stylized illustrations mainly focusing on human figures. My style is heavily influenced by Japanese animations, games, and popular media. The main genres included in my works are fantasy, sci-fi, modern, and street punkI satisfy the demands and desires of my clients whilst producing quality work.
